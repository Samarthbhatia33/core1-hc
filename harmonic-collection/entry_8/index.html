STARTER POKÉMON // 最初のポケモン

At the beginning of their quest, Trainers are given a starter Pokémon or first partner Pokémon. This Pokémon will be used to battle the first wild Pokémon that the Trainer encounters. In the core series games, it is said that the starter Pokémon are extremely rare in the wild, giving the player a reason why the Pokémon not chosen can't be found in the wild in most of the games.
