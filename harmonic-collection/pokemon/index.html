STARTER POKÉMON // 最初のポケモン

At the beginning of their quest, Trainers are given a starter Pokémon or first partner Pokémon. This Pokémon will be used to battle the first wild Pokémon that the Trainer encounters. In the core series games, it is said that the starter Pokémon are extremely rare in the wild, giving the player a reason why the Pokémon not chosen can't be found in the wild in most of the games.

FIRST GEN

ABRA KADABRA!

Abra is a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. Its Pokédex entry states that "this strange fellow sleeps 16 hours a day." It uses its psychic powers while it sleeps. The contents of Abra’s dreams affect the powers that the Pokémon wields. It evolves into Kadabra starting at level 16, which evolves into Alakazam when traded, or when exposed to a Linking Cord in Legends: Arceus.
